      - To enable you to access our Websites and Services and maintain their security and integrity;

* When you open and use the Sober app:
   * What happens:
      - Your device will connect to Cloudflare, our CDN provider, then reach our servers.
      - Sober opens the Roblox app, so your device will connect to Roblox services.
      - Sober processes and collects the bare minimum required to maintain compatibility and security.
   * Third parties:
      - Cloudflare: https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/
      - Roblox: https://en.help.roblox.com/hc/en-us/articles/115004630823-Roblox-Privacy-and-Cookie-Policy
   * Data processed:
      - Roblox is independent of Sober and has its own data collection and processing policy. When you interact
        with Roblox services through Sober, you're interacting with Roblox through our compatibility layer.
        For this reason, Sober does not involve itself in the Roblox app's internal operations.
        Check Roblox's privacy policy to learn how data is collected and used.
      - Just as visiting any other website on the Internet, basic device information and IP addresses
        are temporarily processed to serve the request. This information may be stored in log files by
        us and/or our CDN and server provider.
      - Data required to keep the app stable and secure for everyone, such as crash reports, app process state,
        basic device information and unique device identifiers, active app modules. This information is temporarily processed, 
        usually without ever leaving your device, to keep the app running smoothly and maintain compatibility. Such data may 
        be sent and collected pursuant to the interests laid out below. For example, we might ask you for app diagnostic logs
        on your machine to help debug an issue, use the unique identifiers and process metadata to safeguard our app against
        malicious activity, or track the number of graphics driver-related crashes.
   * Important notice:
      - Your Roblox activity stays between you and Roblox. Sober will *never* track what you play, who you interact with,
        what messages you send, authentication tokens, or what you do on the Roblox platform. We'll *never* collect data
        from your device that is not related to Sober itself.
      - For example, the app may send basic crash statistics, but we'll never send data from other apps, or try to figure out
        what you do on Roblox.
   * Reason for data processing:
      - To enable you to access our Websites and Services and maintain their security and integrity;
      - To provide, maintain, and improve the Websites and Services for all users (like collecting data about crashes to see
        if there's an influx of crashes on a particular version);
      - To provide customer service (like when we ask you for app diagnostic logs on your machine to figure out an issue);
      - To comply with legal obligations and to investigate and prevent unauthorized access to our
        services, and other illegal activities.
   * Legal basis for processing:
      - Fulfillment of a contract (providing you the app and its associated services, making sure the app is secure for everyone);
      - Legitimate interest (providing a safe experience for all users of the app).

   * Reason for data processing:
      - To give you access to the optional Automatic Downloads feature;
